### ❓ Quiz Question

**How many connected components result after performing the following sequence of union operations on a set of 10 items?**

{1–2}, 
{3–4}, 
{5–6}, 
{7–8}, 
{7–9}, 
{2–8}, 
{0–5}, 
{1–9}


- [ ] 1  
- [ ] 2  
- [x] 3 ✅  
- [ ] 4  

### ✅ Correct Answer: 3

The connected components after all union operations are:  
- {0, 5, 6}  
- {3, 4}  
- {1, 2, 7, 8, 9}
